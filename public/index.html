About Xanda

Xanda's Bar and Grill is the brainchild of Jordan Xanda
The original Xanda's was opened in Gotham in 1969 by his father Bruce Xanda.
Jordan Xanda then decided to pay tribute to his father by re−opening it in Singapore.
Now a hearty local bar serving brunch, dinner and cocktails.
Xanda is located at 99 Irving Street (Behind Gotham Alley).
Opened from 12 noon to 12 midnight, Monday to Saturday.
